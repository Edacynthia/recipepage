Simple pancake recipe

Welcome to the ultimate pancake experience! A easy and quick dish, perfect for anymeal. Our easy to follow recipe guarantees light, fluffy pancakes all the time.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Frying: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine buttermilk, milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix well.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  4. Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Grease lightly with butter or cooking spray.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.
